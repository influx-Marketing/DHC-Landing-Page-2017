The Databarracks Data Health Check is an annual snapshot of the IT industry, as described by IT professionals from a variety of backgrounds - from the server room to the board room.

Collectively, the reports offer a fascinating insight into the changing priorities and attitudes towards IT during an incredibly transitional period.
